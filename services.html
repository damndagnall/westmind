Services

Flexible options in Perth and via telehealth — designed for privacy, clarity, and momentum.

In-person sessions (Perth)

Private appointments for adults in the Perth metro area. Ideal if you prefer face-to-face support.

Telehealth (Australia-wide)

Video/phone sessions for clients anywhere in Australia. Same quality care, more flexibility.

Focused or longer-term therapy

Target a specific goal, or work through deeper patterns over time — paced to suit you.

What happens in the first sessions

A clear start — understand what’s happening, agree priorities, and map next steps.

  • ✓ A careful overview of what’s bringing you in, and what you want to change.
  • ✓ Identify maintaining factors (stressors, patterns, triggers, coping strategies).
  • ✓ Agree a plan — practical tools + deeper work where appropriate.
  • ✓ Review progress and adjust to keep therapy useful.
